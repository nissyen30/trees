Common Trees in Kentucky

(and elsewhere in the SouthEast United States)

Do you love to get out and explore the wild beauty of the world around you?

If you do, you have no doubt noticed that the natural world is often populated with nature's CO2 scrubbers which are commonly known as trees. Simply being around these passive cohabitants of our world may be enough for you or you might dismiss these large plants as just another part of the scenery or see them as a potential source of firewood.

But, if you would like to know more about trees, knowing which one you are looking at is a good start as each tree can have widely different characteristics depending on the genus and species to which it belongs.
